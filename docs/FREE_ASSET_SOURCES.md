# Free Asset Sources — Midnight Prowl

Where to find free sprites, audio, and textures for the game. No cost, legal for commercial use.

## Sprite Art & Tilesets

### itch.io (Largest free game art collection)
- **URL:** https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-pixel-art
- **Search terms for our game:**
  - "cat sprite sheet pixel art"
  - "platformer tileset urban night"
  - "2D enemies pixel art"
  - "retro CGA pixel art"
  - "city night background pixel"
  - "heart pixel art"
  - "fish underwater pixel"
  - "spider pixel art enemy"
- **License:** Varies per pack — look for CC0 or "free for commercial use"
- **Why it's best:** Thousands of packs, filtered by free + tag, many specifically for platformers

### OpenGameArt.org (Dedicated free game art archive)
- **URL:** https://opengameart.org
- **Search terms:**
  - "cat character 2d"
  - "platformer tiles"
  - "urban tileset"
  - "night sky background"
  - "dog enemy sprite"
  - "spider enemy"
  - "fish underwater"
  - "bird flying animation"
  - "broom"
  - "heart item"
- **License:** CC0, CC-BY, or GPL (check each asset)
- **Why it's good:** Clear licensing info on every asset, community curated

### Kenney.nl (Professional quality, all CC0)
- **URL:** https://kenney.nl/assets
- **Recommended packs:**
  - "Pixel Platformer" — characters, tiles, platforms
  - "UI Pack" — buttons, joystick graphics, icons
  - "Game Icons" — life hearts, items
  - "Platformer Art Deluxe" — backgrounds, terrain
- **License:** CC0 (public domain — no credit needed, fully commercial)
- **Why it's good:** Consistent style, professional quality, zero restrictions

### Pixel Art Specific
- **Lospec Palette List:** https://lospec.com/palette-list — CGA and retro palettes to match our style
- **Piskel (Free Sprite Editor):** https://www.piskelapp.com — Make your own pixel art in browser
- **Aseprite (Paid but industry standard):** https://www.aseprite.org — $20, best pixel art tool

## License Quick Reference

| License | Use in Game? | Credit Needed? | Sell Game? |
|---------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **CC0** | ✅ | No | ✅ |
| **CC-BY** | ✅ | Yes (add credits screen) | ✅ |
| **CC-BY-SA** | ✅ | Yes + must share derivative art | ✅ (careful) |
| **MIT** | ✅ | Include license file | ✅ |
| **GPL** | ⚠️ | Must open-source entire game | ⚠️ Avoid |
| **No License stated** | ❌ | Don't use — risky | ❌ |

**Rule of thumb:** Only use CC0, CC-BY, or MIT licensed assets. Add a "Credits" screen in the game listing all CC-BY authors.
